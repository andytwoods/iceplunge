Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

1. Introduction

The Cold Cognition Project ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you participate in our community-led citizen science study.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our project:

  • Personal Identifiers: Email address (used for account management and notifications).
  • Demographic Data: Age, sex, years of cold-water experience (collected at sign-up).
  • Cognitive Performance Data: Results from tasks measuring reaction time, attention, and processing speed.
  • Contextual Data (Covariates): Information about your plunges (temperature, duration), sleep, caffeine/alcohol intake, and mood.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide you with a personal dashboard to track your performance.
  • Conduct pseudonymised research on the effects of cold-water immersion.
  • Generate aggregated, anonymised summaries for the community.
  • Improve the tasks and user experience of the project.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data in the following ways:

  • Aggregated Results: We share anonymised, group-level findings with the community and in research publications.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law to comply with a legal obligation.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. All data is stored and analysed against a random participant ID rather than your direct identifiers.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data via your personal dashboard.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time.
  • Request the deletion of your account and raw data via your account settings.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at research@coldcognition.org.
